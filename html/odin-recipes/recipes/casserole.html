Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

This baked sweet potato casserole topped with a delicious pecan topping is so good and creamy. Try this recipe during festivals and it will surely become your new tradition!

Preparation Details

Prep Time:
20 mins
Cook Time:
45 mins
Servings:
12

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Pecans

Steps

  1. Cook and mash the sweet potatoes.
  2. Mix the casserole ingredients and transfer it to a baking dish.
  3. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the sweet potato mixture.
  4. Bake until the topping is lightly browned.
