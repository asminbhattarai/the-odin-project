Easy Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves lots of time. Try it tonight!

Preparation Details

Prep Time:
15 mins
Cook Time:
1 hr 15 mins
Servings:
12

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses (Cottage, Mozarella, Parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings (Dried parsley, salt, black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 mins.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
